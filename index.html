Stages of creating candles

Creating a candle from soy wax can consist of the following stages:

  1. Preparation of materials: small fragments of soy wax, candle wick, aromas, dyes and other additional materials.

  2. Preparation of wax: melting soy wax in a melting device to the desired temperature. Addition of flavorings and dyes if necessary.

  3. Form preparation: choosing a form for a candle and preparing it for filling with wax.

  4. Preparing the wick: Inserting the wick into the mold and securing it so that it is evenly tensioned.

  5. Preparation of materials: small fragments of soy wax, candle wick, aromas, dyes and other additional materials.

  6. Preparation of wax: melting soy wax in a melting device to the desired temperature. Addition of flavorings and dyes if necessary.

  7. Filling the mold: pour the wax into the mold until it is completely filled. Wait until it hardens.
